Publications

NOREF’s publications aim to provide knowledge and analysis of high quality and relevance to the fields of mediation, conflict resolution and the wider field of peacebuilding. These texts, spanning across a wide range of topics and conflicts, are currently categorized as either in-depth Reports or the shorter NOREF Briefs. In addition, our archive also holds other categories which have now been discontinued. All texts are written by NOREF staff or commissioned from an extensive network of experts worldwide.
